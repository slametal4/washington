2024–25 Washington Capitals season

The 2024–25 Washington Capitals season is the 51st season (50th season of play) for the National Hockey League (NHL) franchise that was established on June 11, 1974.

On March 20, 2025, the Capitals became the first team to clinch a playoff spot and their second consecutive berth with a win over the Philadelphia Flyers.

On April 6, captain Alexander Ovechkin scored his 895th NHL regular season goal, breaking the record for most scored goals held by Wayne Gretzky.

On April 8, the Capitals clinched the Metropolitan Division title after the Carolina Hurricanes lost to the Buffalo Sabres.

On April 10, the Capitals clinched the best record in the Eastern Conference with a win over the Carolina Hurricanes.
